Fable Psychology offers comprehensive assessments for gender affirming care under the World Professional Association for Transgender Health (WPATH) model. Ashley is a psychologist with a special interest in working with the LGBTQIA+ community and has experience in writing a range of gender-affirming care letters.

Ashley approaches this work with respect, care, and an understanding that seeking gender-affirming treatment can be both exciting and vulnerable. The goal is to provide a thorough, affirming, and supportive assessment process.

Who is it for?

Transgender, non-binary and other gender-diverse adults who are preparing for gender-affirming care, such as hormone therapy, surgical procedures (top surgery, bottom surgery, etc). Typically people who are at the stage of seeking this care have already socially transitioned in some aspects, but it is not a requirement to fully socially transition.

Purpose of assessment

An assessment is often requested by doctors or surgeons in order to provide gender affirming care. In general, doctors want to be assured that;

  • There is a current diagnosis of gender dysphoria.
  • Their patient has the capacity to consent to the specific treatment.
  • Explore any mental health factors that may be relevant to care planning.

Please note that this is an assessment service and not an ongoing therapeutic service.

Process of assessment

Please complete the contact form to get started. An initial 90 minute session will be conducted via telehealth to gather information for the assessment. Following this, you will be asked to complete some psychometric assessments in your own time. After there has been some time to interpret the assessments, a follow-up 60 minutes telehealth session will occur to provide feedback and discuss any follow-up questions. Then the report will be written, and sent to you for your confirmation before being finalised.
